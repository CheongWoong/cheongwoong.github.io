I am currently pursuing my PhD at the Graduate School of AI, KAIST, affiliated with the <a href="http://sailab.kaist.ac.kr/">SAIL</a>. My PhD advisor is <a href="http://sailab.kaist.ac.kr/members/jaesik/">Jaesik Choi</a>. I am interested in natural language processing, deep learning, reinforcement learning and explainable AI (XAI). My primary interest lies in how knowledge is encoded and processed for reasoning in language models, along with how knowledge can be extracted. I am actively engaged in two research directions: (1) analyzing knowledge inside language models and (2) improving language models by controlling or augmenting knowledge.
